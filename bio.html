A Bit About Myself

Hello, I'm Sulaimon Okikiola Olanrewaju (in full). I'm writing to you right now from Florida in the United States. As the Chief Executive Officer of the software development, website development, and data analytics business Keyventki Company Limited, I oversee all of these operations.

The previous 10 years have been filled with dedication, perseverance, and passion combined with learning and earning.

I completed my journey by earning a Master's in Data Science as well as a Computer Science degree from Kibo School. After working for three startups and four of the best IT corporations in the world, I decided to set up a business where my team and I create software to solve problems in real life.

Even though I've faced many challenges in life, I've persevered because I want to avoid having to reflect on the past.

Right now, I feel great because I can pay my bills without stress, help people in need as I see fit, and obtain anything I want whenever I want it without having to complain.

I can only advise my present self the same encouragement I used to give it. ‘Keep going. You can do it!’
